FAQ

These the common questions event organisers want to know

Can I change my profile information?

Yes just go to your profile by clicking the profile icon in upper right corner in settings page

Where do I see the events I made?

You can go to your home tab and see all latest events created by you.

Can I buy tickets?

Not as an event organiser but you can create a new account with a different email and sign up as a customer and use that account to buy tickets

Where do I scan customer qr code?

You can go to your qr scanner tab and press scanner button which use your camera to scan tickets.

How can I contact TicketMarshall?

You can go to your settings and go to contact page where you see how to contact the TicketMarshall Team by email.
